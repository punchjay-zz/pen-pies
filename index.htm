Peninsula Pies is a mobile wood fired pizza co. serving traditional Italian pies with a PNW flavor, sourcing local and seasonal ingredients.

We are based in Kingston, WA. Located at Downpour Brewing. We can come to your party, wedding, reunion, whatever!

We want to help you celebrate!
