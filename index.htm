Peninsula Pies is a mobile wood fired pizza company serving traditional Napoli style pizza with a Pacific Northwest flare, sourcing local and seasonal ingredients.

We are Located at Downpour Brewing a block from the ferry in Kingston, WA.

Yet we can go anywhere! To your party, wedding, reunion, etc. We will work with you to create the menu you want for your event. We want to help you celebrate!
