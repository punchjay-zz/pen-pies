Our goal is to nourish our neighborhood and visitors with Italian inspired food and drink. The small deli case is filled with local and international cheese and charcuterie. We have a small menu, including fresh pasta that changes with the seasons and source many of our ingredients locally.

The cafe is a place for friends to gather or to just stop in and take something to go while providing excellent customer service and knowledge of the products we carry.

Our Pizza

Our every day menu consists of a few classics, plus a specialty pizza or two that changes weekly.
